Standardization system for the educational metadata for the corpus:

No Schooling
Some Primary School (Kinder through 7th)
Completed Primary School (Finished 8th)
Some High School (Between 9th - 11th)
Completed High School (12th)
Some College (Associates Degree, incomplete Bachelor's, community college, under 4 years of college)
Completed College ("college", "university", Completed Bachelor's, 4 years college or greater)
Some Advanced Education (Some Master's or Doctorate Education)
Completed Advanced Education (Completed Master's or Completed Doctorate)
Unknown (put age instead of grade, non-academic licenses, blanks)
